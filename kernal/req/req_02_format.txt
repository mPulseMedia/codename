req_02_format

q_2a__ format          - Formatting requirements
q_2a_1    text         - Style text elements:
                          - Make "hello" text red
                          - Make "world" text blue
q_2a_2    circle       - Add circular container:
                          - Draw circle around "hello world" text
                          - Create vertical shadow effect:
                            a) Shadow is a smaller version of the circle
                            b) Shadow is offset downward significantly
                            c) Bottom edge of circle appears to rest on shadow
                            d) Shadow creates illusion of circle floating above surface
                            e) Shadow has soft, blurred edges for natural appearance
                            f) Shadow appears as a distinct layer beneath the circle
                          - Implement hover effect:
                            a) When mouse hovers over circle, shadow moves down slightly
                            b) Creates illusion of circle lifting from surface
                            c) Maintains shadow's smaller size and shape
                            d) Smooth transition for natural movement
                            e) Shadow maintains soft edges during hover
q_2a_3    codename     - Required codenames for formatting:
                          circle - Circular container element
                          circle_shadow - Shadow beneath circle element
